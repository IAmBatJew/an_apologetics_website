Is the Universe Designed?

There are countless fine-tuned aspects of the universe.
These are things that if they are off by the smallest of fractions from their current values, the universe could not support life.
Most of us know that we are in the goldie locks zone of our solar system,
but did you know we are in the goldie locks zone of our solar system?
Not only that, but Earth's gravitational pull had to be accurate within a range of 1 in 1040.
If it wasn't the moon would either crash into earth or veer off into empty space.
There are about 50 fine-tuned characteristics of the universe
and about 800 fine-tuned characteristics of our solar system.
